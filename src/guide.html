How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Compose an email to capassoa2922@gmail.com.
  2. In the subject line, write: "Account Deletion: [Your TaskFroge Account Email]".
  3. In the body of the email, include the following information:
    • Your TaskFroge account email address.
    • The reason for deleting your account (optional).
  4. Send the email.
  5. Once your request is received, our team will process it accordingly.
